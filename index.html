Our Deep Motivation

Why does ECOGREEN exist?

Lebanon is world-renowned for its beautiful old wineries in the Bekaa and historic olive orchards in Koura. However, processing these crops leaves thousands of tons of wet agricultural waste behind.

🔥

The Waste Crisis

Instead of being reused, wet grape mash and compressed olive pulp are often left out to rot or disposed of via unsafe open-air farm fires, creating toxic greenhouse smoke.

💧

Protecting Soils

When massive batches of acidic agricultural waste degrade on open ground, rainwater runoff washes concentrated residues into underground rivers, polluting village wells.

⚡

Energy Security

Many rural areas face electricity shortages. By building neighborhood biogas networks, we produce a safe, renewable power source controlled completely by the community.

Anaerobic Digestion

Nature's Clean Recycling Loop

How the System Works

Converting Waste to Fuel

Once you bring your agricultural waste items to our public collection hubs, the material is transferred directly to sealed, oxygen-free biomass digesters located near the fields.

Naturally occurring beneficial micro-organisms break down the matter over a few weeks. This organic cycle outputs **biogas** (which can be used for electricity and heating) and **nutrient-rich bio-fertilizer**, which is given right back to local farmers to look after the soil!
